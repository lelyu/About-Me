Is College Worth It?

Created in Fall 2022 during a Data Visualization course, this project employs Tableau, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to illuminate the trends in college tuition and its impact on income. It offers interactive visualizations that reveal changes in tuition costs over time, differences in regional education expenses, and the economic benefits of a college degree. This concise yet comprehensive analysis serves as a valuable resource for students, educators, and policymakers, highlighting the significant role of higher education in shaping financial futures.
