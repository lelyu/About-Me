What Model Best Predicts Revenue for Domino's Pizza?

Written as a final project for the course Financial Forecasting in Fall 2023, this paper explores the relationship between Domino's Pizza's revenue and its various financial and non-financial factors. The paper also compares the performance of different models in predicting revenue, such as between Multiple Regression, ARIMA, Exponential Smoothing, etc. The models are iteratively developed using SPSS by IBM.

A python automation script (availible in my GitHub Page) was developed to automate the process in developing the best ARIMA model.
